## 💡 Outreach Tips for Education Sector

1. **Decision Makers to Target:**
   - Проректор по цифровизации / IT Director
   - Начальник IT-отдела
   - Проректор по учебной работе (for LMS)

2. **Pain Points to Address:**
   - Outdated LMS systems (many still use Platonus/Moodle poorly)
   - Manual administrative processes
   - Poor mobile experience for students
   - Integration between systems

3. **Timing:**
   - Budget planning: September-October
   - Tender season: January-March
   - Summer: IT infrastructure upgrades
